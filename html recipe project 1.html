GRANDMA'S LEMON MERINGUE PIE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Cub of white suger
  • 2 tea spoon of all purpose flour
  • 3 tea spoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 tea spoon of a salt
  • 11/2 cups of water
  • 2 lemon juice and zested
  • 2tea spoon butter
  • 4 egg yorks beaten
  • 1(9inch)pie crust baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tea spoon of white suger

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven:preheat the oven to 350 degrees f(175 degrees C)
  2. Make a lemon filling:make lemon filling in a medium source pan
    • whisk together one cup of suger,flour,cornerstarch and salt
    • stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest
    • cook over medium high beat stirring frequently untill the mixture comes to a boil
    • stirr in butter
    • place egg yorks in a bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of mixture of suger
    • whisk egg york mixture back to the remaining suger mixture
    • bring to a boil and continue to cookwhile stirring constantly untill thick
    • remove from the heat
    • pour filling into baked pastry shell
  3. Make a maringue:make a maringue in a large glass or a metal bowl
    • whip egg whites untill foamy
    • add suger gradually,and continue to whip untill stiff peaks form
    • spread the meringue into pie, sealing the edges at the crust
  4. Bake in a preheated oven:Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes, or untill the meringue is golden brown
